10 Nostalgic 90s Toys Making a Comeback in 2026

The 90s are back in full force — and collectors are paying big for the originals. Here are 10 toys that are hot again right now:

  1. Tamagotchi — Virtual pets ruled the playground. Reissues are $20–$40, originals (working) $50–$200+.
  2. Furby — The creepy-cute comeback king. Vintage $30–$150, new re-releases $40–$80.
  3. Polly Pocket — Compact playsets are ruling again. Originals $15–$100, reissues $20–$50.
  4. Beanie Babies — Rare ones still fetch hundreds (some thousands). Common ones $10–$50.
  5. Pokémon Cards — First edition packs and holos are gold. Vintage booster packs $50–$500+, modern reprints $10–$30.
  6. Power Rangers Morphers & Figures — Iconic 90s action. Vintage Zords $40–$200, reissues $30–$60.
  7. Sky Dancers — Flying dolls that drove parents crazy. Originals $20–$100, re-releases $25–$50.
  8. Lite-Brite — Glow-in-the-dark art is timeless. Vintage sets $30–$80, modern versions $20–$40.
  9. Skip-It — The ankle toy that was everywhere. Vintage $15–$60, reissues $20–$35.
  10. Pogs / Milk Caps — The ultimate 90s recess game. Rare slammers $10–$100+, full sets $20–$80.
